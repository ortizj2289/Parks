Erwin Park

Erwin Park is one of the newer parks to me. Now that I live on the opposite side of McKinney, I've been taking advantage of what Erwin Park has to offer. You can find more information HERE.

Erwin Park has some unique ammenities not available anywhere else. If you are a fan of camping, this park is for you. I have not personally taken advantage of this feature but I do go on the occasional hike there and I have seen the numerous camping spots available. If you want to spend a weekend in a tent but not want to deal with complete isolation, you should definitely give this a try. You would only be 15 minutes away from the nearest Walmart.

Erwin Park seems to be the most "outdoorsy" to me with its long hiking trails that can be over 8 miles long, and abundant wildlife. It has a bicycle trail for the cycling enthusiasts, not available anywhere else. I think it has less available than other parks but because of its size, its easy to spend alot of time here exploring nature. Because of its simplicity and size, I give Erwin Park 4 out of 5 stars!
