McKinney's Public Parks

This website is dedicated to ranking some of the local parks located in McKinney, Tx.
McKinney has over 45 public parks but this website will only rank some of my favorite and most familiar to me.

The parks included range mostly from around the Downtown McKinney area since they are the ones I visit most often. Most of them have similar features but each have their own style.
