Towne Lake

Towne Lake is a classic spot here in Mckinney. To me, it goes hand in hand with Historic Downtown McKinney. You can find more information HERE.

Towne Lake has seen many changes over the years. Most notably, it once allowed residents to tour its waters via swan boats. That amenity has been long gone but has recently been replaced with a kayak dock.

Towne Lake has lots of features that make it well-rounded park. From its 1 mile paved trail around the lake and volleyball courts, there is plenty to do here. Other sports include the softball field, disc golf course, and horseshoe courts. Towne Lake is also where fishing tournaments take place.

With all of these ammenities available at this location, I give Towne Lake 3.5 stars out of five!
